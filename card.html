Grizzly Bear

You can recognize grizzly bears by the hump on their back.
Hump is made of muscles.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus arctos
  • Average Size: 600lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25 years in the wild
  • Habitat: From dense forests to subalpine meadows,
    open plains and arctic tundra.

Grizzly bears are powerful, top-of-the-food-chain predators,
yet much of their diet consists of nuts, berries, fruit, leaves,
and roots. Bears also eat other animals, from rodents to moose.
